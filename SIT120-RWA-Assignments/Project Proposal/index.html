Pomodoro Time Management System

This website will provide its users the ability to track and manage their workload while utilizing maximum focus. Users will be able to add tasks and track their workload and the system will automatically notify users to take necessary breaks to utilize maximus focus. App will use the pomodoro technique which is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillio in the 1980s. The system is proven to give its users the maximum productivity without adhering to painful discipline methods.
